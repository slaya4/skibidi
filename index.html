How to Switch Health Insurance Plans Without Losing Coverage

Switching health insurance plans can be stressful, especially when continuity of coverage matters. With proper planning, it is possible to transition smoothly without exposing yourself to unnecessary risk.

Understand Enrollment Periods

Most insurance changes must be made during open enrollment or after qualifying life events. Understanding these rules helps prevent coverage gaps.

Compare Plan Details Carefully

Look beyond monthly premiums. Deductibles, provider networks, and out-of-pocket limits are equally important.

Confirm Coverage Dates

Always ensure your new policy begins before your old one ends.
